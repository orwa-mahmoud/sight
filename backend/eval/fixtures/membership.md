# Membership and pricing

We offer three plans. The Hot Desk is $99 per month for unreserved seating in the
common area.

The Dedicated Desk is $199 per month and gives you your own fixed desk with a
locker.

The Private Office starts at $499 per month for a lockable room. Dedicated Desk
and Private Office plans include 24/7 access.

If you just want to try us out, a day pass is $25 and includes all common-area
amenities.
